How AI Uses Your Lower Secondary Syllabus

Artificial Intelligence runs entirely on the core principles of Pemikiran Komputasional that you study in school.

ASK: Pengecaman Corak

1. Pattern Recognition

Machine Learning models analyze huge datasets to find hidden textures and trends that humans might miss.

Why it matters: this is how AI spots repeated shapes, sound patterns, or behaviour trends without being told every rule in advance.
ASK: Leraian

2. Decomposition

To analyze complex data, an AI breaks real-world environments down into independent processing streams (features).

Why it matters: splitting a problem into smaller parts makes the system easier to train, debug, and improve.
ASK: Peniskalaan

3. Abstraction

AI models practice Abstraction by filtering out background noise, random spikes, or visual glitch patterns to focus solely on crucial data properties.

Why it matters: abstraction helps the model ignore distractions and keep the signals that actually influence the result.
RBT: Carta Alir

4. Dynamic Control Structures

Instead of hardcoding a fixed, permanent "YES/NO" flowchart choice, an AI pipeline paths itself dynamically using data pattern weights.

Why it matters: AI can adjust the next action based on what it detects, instead of following one fixed path every time.
