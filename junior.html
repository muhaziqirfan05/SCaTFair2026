What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is when computers learn to do things that normally need human thinking. It also can define as a machine that can make a decissions.

AI can:

  • Answer questions
  • Recognize pictures and voices
  • Recommend videos or songs
  • Help people learn new things

Everyday Uses

Examples of AI you might know:

  • Voice assistant like Siri or Google Assistant
  • Youtube recommendations
  • NPC behavior in video games
  • Face filters on Tiktok
  • Face ID
  • Translation Apps

How do AI learn?

AI can learn by analyzing large amounts of data and identifying patterns. This process is called machine learning.

Example:

  • If we show an AI thousands of cat pictures, it learns what cats look like.
  • Later, it can guess whether a new picture is a cat or not.

Good Uses of AI?

AI can help people:

  • Learn faster
  • Stay safer
  • Save time
  • Solve problems
  • Help people with disabilities
